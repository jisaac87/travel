A beatiful country, a place full of life that you dont want to leave

The is not enough ways to describe our experience with this beautiful country, from the beautiful scenic locations, the delicious local cuisine, nightlife that is literally full of life. Thailand is a place where you will have an experience that you will never forget.

Tasty cuisine

Plenty of options for the meat eaters to those that love trying the authentic taste of the local culture. Street vendors are everywhere serving dishes for breakfast, lunch, and dinner with food being perfect to have while your busy shopping or taking in the amazing sites and sounds in Thailand.

Elephant Sanctuary

The one we personally went to was the Elephant Jungle Sanctuary where we spent time with Natalie and Shao, we chose this from the vast of sanctuary available as elephants in this sanctuary are rescues. The elephants is beautiful creatures who in a weird way feel their gentle soul.
